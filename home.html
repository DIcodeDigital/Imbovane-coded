Who Are We?

Imbovane

Imbovane is derived from the isiXhosa word for “ant” — a symbol of strength, diligence, and unity. Just like the ant, we believe in the quiet power of collective discipline, strategic effort, and unwavering willpower. These values guide every project we undertake.

“It’s not enough to be busy. So are the ants. The question is: what are we busy about?” – Henry David Thoreau

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Leonardo Barnes, Imbovane Business and Strategy empowers entrepreneurs and small businesses to grow sustainably through planning, coaching, and strategic transformation. Our approach combines analytical precision with creative vision — ensuring every client’s journey is structured, measurable, and deeply aligned with their long-term purpose.
